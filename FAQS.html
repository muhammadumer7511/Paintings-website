Artwork Care

How should I clean my painting?
Gently dust with a soft brush. For deep cleaning, consult our professional conservation services.
What's the ideal environment for oil paintings?
Maintain 40-50% humidity and stable temperatures between 18-24°C (64-75°F).
How to protect from UV damage?
Use UV-filtering glass and avoid direct sunlight. We offer protective glazing options.
